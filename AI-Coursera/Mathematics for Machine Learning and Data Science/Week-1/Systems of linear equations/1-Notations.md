Here’s a **clean and simple table** of your mathematical notations for Machine Learning and Data 

|**Notation**|**Meaning / Description**|
|---|---|
|**A, B, C**|Capital letters represent **matrices**|
|**u, v, w**|Lowercase letters represent **vectors**|
|**A (m × n)** or **A of size m×n**|Matrix **A** has **m rows** and **n columns**|
|**Aᵀ**|**Transpose** of matrix A|
|**vᵀ**|**Transpose** of vector v|
|**A⁻¹**|**Inverse** of matrix A|
|**det(A)**|**Determinant** of matrix A|
|**AB**|**Matrix multiplication** of matrices A and B|
|**u · v** or **⟨u, v⟩**|**Dot product** of vectors u and v|
|**ℝ**|Set of **real numbers** (e.g., 0, −0.642, 2, 3.456)|
|**ℝ²**|Set of **2-dimensional vectors** (e.g., v = [1 3]ᵀ)|
|**ℝⁿ**|Set of **n-dimensional vectors**|
|**v ∈ ℝ²**|Vector v is an **element of** ℝ²|
|**|v|
|**|v|
|**T : ℝ² → ℝ³**|**Transformation** T maps a vector from ℝ² to ℝ³|
|**T(v) = w**|Transformation **T** maps vector **v** to vector **w**|
